Welcome to Our NGO

Helping Hands NGO works for education, women empowerment, healthcare, environment protection, disaster relief, and child welfare.

Our Mission

To improve the quality of life of underprivileged communities through education, health, and social development.

Our Vision

A society where every individual has equal opportunity to live with dignity, education, and healthcare.

Our Values

Compassion, transparency, accountability, teamwork, integrity, and sustainable development.

Our Programs

Creating sustainable impact through multiple initiatives.

Education

Free education and digital literacy programs.

Healthcare

Medical camps and awareness programs.

Environment

Tree plantation and cleanliness drives.

Community

Helping families during emergencies.
